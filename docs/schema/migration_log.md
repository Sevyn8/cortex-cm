## e59f62d5037d — Step 2.2a: NULLIF wrapper on RLS GUC reads (2026-05-02)

Commit: c68219e

Modified:
- RLS policies `tenants_self_access`, `tenant_users_tenant_isolation`, `org_nodes_tenant_isolation`, `stores_tenant_isolation`, `user_role_assignments_tenant_isolation` on all 5 multi-tenant tables: wraps `current_setting('app.tenant_id', TRUE)` in `NULLIF(..., '')` before casting to `uuid`. Rationale: Postgres registers placeholder GUCs as the empty string `''` after the first `set_config` call on a connection; casting `''::uuid` raises and crashes every reused pooled connection past its first transaction.
